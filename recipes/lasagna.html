Lasagna

Description

The lasagna is a typical Italian dish. One of the most beloved comfort foods all around the world.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Minced beef

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Cook the minced beef with tomato sauce
  3. Mount a layer of pasta
  4. Lay down a layer of beef with tomato sauce
